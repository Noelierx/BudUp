The BudUp Story

BudUp was created during the Athena Hackathon in november 2021 by the Ctrl alt compete team.

The idea was to create a platform for remote teams to work together and build a culture of belonging.

The team was inspired by the idea of a remote team, where the team members could work together and build a culture of belonging.
